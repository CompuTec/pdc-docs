| 12  | @CT_PF_MOR4  |             Scraps in Manufacturing Order             |
| 13  | @CT_PF_MOR2  |           WIP Items in Manufacturing Order            |

| 27  | @CT_PF_WDC1  |                 Weight document lines                 |
| 28  | @CT_PF_OWLG  |                      Weight log                       |
| 29  |     OHEM     |                 Employee Master Data                  |
